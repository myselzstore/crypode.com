✦ Our Story

Built by someone living it, not just advising it

Crypode was born from a simple frustration — most financial advice is written by people who have never truly struggled. Who have never watched 50% of their salary vanish into debt repayments while six children depend on them.

This is a space for honest conversations about money in the Gulf context — where banking rules are different, family obligations are real, and faith shapes how we think about wealth and provision.

No judgment. No false promises. Just real guidance from someone walking the same road.

"I tried to find advice that understood my reality — the debt, the family, the Gulf system. I couldn't find it. So I decided to write it."
C
The Founder Technical Supervisor · Father of Six · Muscat, Oman
