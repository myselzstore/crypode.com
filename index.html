✦ Our Story

Built by someone living it, not just advising it

Crypode started from a simple frustration: most financial advice comes from people who’ve never truly struggled. Who’ve never watched half their salary disappear into debt while six children depend on them.

This is a space for honest money conversations rooted in the Gulf — where banking rules are different, family obligations are real, and faith shapes our view of wealth and provision.

No judgment. No get‑rich‑quick fantasies. Just real guidance from someone walking the same road.

"I looked for advice that understood my reality — the debt, the family, the Gulf system. I couldn't find it. So I started writing it myself."
C
The FounderTechnical Supervisor · Father of Six · Muscat, Oman
