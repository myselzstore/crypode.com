Zakat Calculator

Calculate your annual Zakat obligation across cash, gold, investments, and business assets. Based on the 2.5% rate applied to net zakatable wealth above the nisab threshold.

☪

What is Nisab? The minimum threshold of wealth that makes Zakat obligatory — equal to the value of 85g of gold or 595g of silver. If your total zakatable wealth exceeds this and has been held for one lunar year (hawl), Zakat is due.
