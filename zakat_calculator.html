Calculate your annual Zakat obligation across cash, gold, investments, and business assets. Based on the 2.5% rate applied to net zakatable wealth above the nisab threshold.

☪
What is Nisab? Nisab is the minimum threshold of wealth that makes Zakat obligatory. It equals the value of 85g of gold or 595g of silver. If your total zakatable wealth exceeds this threshold and you have held it for one lunar year (hawl), Zakat is due.
